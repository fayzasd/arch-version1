About

Our Services

Complete Architectural, Interior, Landscape and Urban Design Services by Council of Architects (COA) licensed professionals Construction supervision, Structural and MEP designs are also included in our service.
We have expertise in residential, commercial, and Urban Design Projects and doing Various Project across India and UAE.

Our Philosophy

We aim to build beautiful, progressive buildings and environments that should be excellent in terms of design functionality and comfort

Our Mission

Provide quality design for affordable price

Our Team

3dor concepts is an interdisciplinary, architecture and design firm started in 2013 by three enthusiastic young Architects, graduates from department of architecture-CET Trivandrum. Founded on the belief that design has the power to transform lives and enhance communities. We have expertise in residential, commercial, interior, landscape designs and also in mobility, product and graphic designs.. Now we are growing overseas and Clientele extends to UAE also.
